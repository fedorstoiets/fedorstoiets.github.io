Work meaning

When your job is fine, but your life feels flat.

A short educational resource. Replace this starter draft with your full blog article when ready.

A job can be stable and still not feel connected to your values.

Before quitting, study what feels flat: the role, the environment, the pace, the people, or the story you tell yourself about success.

The goal is not always a dramatic change. Sometimes it is a better relationship with work and a richer life outside it.
