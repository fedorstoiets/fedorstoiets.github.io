Biography

Meet Fedor Stoietskyi.

I help people make sense of transition, pressure, identity, and meaning—especially when life looks fine from the outside but feels confusing inside.

Image suggestion Professional headshot: warm, calm, direct eye contact, simple natural background.

My approach

Practical support with clinical depth.

My work is grounded in curiosity, structure, and respect for the full context of your life. That means your culture, family history, work, immigration story, hobbies, values, and stress patterns all matter.

I do not believe people need generic advice when they are overwhelmed. They need a clear space to think, a guide who can notice patterns, and tools that work in daily life.

  • Education and training connected to Boston University and Boston College.
  • Experience across children, adolescents, adults, older adults, hospice care, and neurodiverse clients.
  • Remote, flexible support based in Boston, Massachusetts.

Experience
